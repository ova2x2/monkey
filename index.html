What is a Monkey?

Monkeys have large brains and are known for their inquisitiveness and intelligence.

Brain development, combined with the freeing of the hands and well-developed vision, allows them a great latitude of activity.

Most are good at solving complex problems and learning from experience, but they do not quite reach the cognitive levels of great apes..
